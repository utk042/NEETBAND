# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Students following the CBSE Class 3-12 syllabus (particularly Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics) who are looking for engaging, auditory learning methods to improve retention and reduce reading fatigue.

## Product Purpose
NeetBand converts complex academic concepts into professionally produced, catchy study songs paired with integrated MCQs for active recall.

## Brand Personality
Confident, editorial, engaging, educational, grounded.

## Anti-references
- SaaS "hero-metric" templates
- AI-generated "ghost cards" (glassmorphism with diffuse glow)
- Overstimulating, constantly shimmering effects
- Tiny, tracked-out kickers above every section (the standard "AI slop" template)

## Design Principles
- Flatten depth and use solid color blocks with distinct borders.
- Typographic discipline: ensure `text-balance` on all headings and avoid gradient text.
- Intentionally toned-down motion: reduce uniform GSAP entry animations and endless shimmer.
- Clean container hierarchy over blurred overlays.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Proper contrast ratios (avoiding light gray text on tinted white backgrounds).
- Reduced motion accommodations where animations exist.
- Clear readability.
